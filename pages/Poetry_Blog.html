Sri's Poems

1

Aatma Ghosha

Mee kosam puttam

Meeru cheppinattu perigaam..

Samajam kosam pelli chesukunnam..

Samvidhanam cheppinattu brathukuthunnam..

Chuttu pakkala valla kosam pillalni kannam...

Ippudu Jeevithantham valla kosamey alochisthunnam...

Ah alochanalloo.. Peddayyi poyyam....

Vayasayipoyyi chachipoyyam..

Maro janma maaku undho ledho telidu kaani... Ee janma lo matram memu lemu...

Jeevitham antha kashtapaddam... Eam saadincham.. Chachipothey samvatsaram tarwatha okaru iddariki Thappa inka eavariki gurthu kudaa lemu...

Maa jeevitham lo theeskunna prathi nirnayam mimmalni drushti lo pettukuney theeskunnam...

Thamasha yenti antey meeru maa gurinchi mee jeevitham motham lo matladindhi alochinchindhi maha Ayithe oka ganta untundhi...ledha rendu gantalu... ah ganta Sepu mee matallo... alochanallo manchi ga undalani maa 100 samvatsaralaa.. jeevithanni decide chesey nirnayalu theeskunnam..

Mimmalni Thappu pattatledhu.. Meeru ee circle lo irukkuni unnavalley...
